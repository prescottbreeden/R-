R+ Training Methods

Fixed Ratio

Fixed ratio schedules require a certain number of operant responses (e.g., 10 responses) to produce the next reinforcer.
Variable Ratio

Variable ratio schedules are the "slot-machines" of reinforcement: it could take 1 response to produce a reinforcer, or it could take 20.
